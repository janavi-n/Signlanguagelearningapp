## 📱 Welcome to SignSpeak!

## 🚀 Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Launch the App**
   - You'll see our beautiful splash screen with the SignSpeak logo
   - Wait 2-3 seconds for automatic progression

2. **Onboarding Experience** (3 screens)
   - **Screen 1:** Learn about interactive video lessons
   - **Screen 2:** Discover AI-powered practice features
   - **Screen 3:** Understand our mission to break communication barriers
   - Tap "Next" to progress or "Skip" to jump ahead

3. **Create Your Account**
   - Choose from three options:
     - 📧 **Email/Password:** Traditional signup
     - 🌐 **Google Sign-In:** Quick OAuth login
     - 👤 **Guest Mode:** Try without creating an account
   - Fill in your details and tap "Sign In" or "Create Account"

## 🤖 AI Practice Screen

Real-time sign recognition and feedback.

### How It Works

1. **Setup**
   - Camera interface appears
   - Current sign prompt shows at top
   - Difficulty badge displays
   - Progress tracker updates

2. **Recording**
   - Tap "Start Recording"
   - Position hand in camera frame
   - Hold sign steady
   - Wait for AI analysis (2 seconds)

3. **Feedback**
   - **✓ Correct:** Green overlay, accuracy percentage
   - **✗ Incorrect:** Red overlay, suggestions shown
   - Accuracy score displays (0-100%)

4. **AI Suggestions** (if incorrect)
   - Center hand in frame
   - Ensure fingers are visible
   - Hold position steady
   - Review lesson if needed

5. **Accuracy Meter**
   - Visual progress bar
   - Color-coded (green = good, orange = needs work)
   - Performance feedback text
   - Score interpretation

6. **Actions**
   - **Retry:** Try the same sign again
   - **Next Sign:** Move to next prompt
   - **View Lesson:** Review instructions
   - **Finish:** Complete practice session

### Practice Tips
- 💡 Use good lighting
- 💡 Keep hand clearly visible
- 💡 Match the demonstrated position
- 💡 Practice consistently
- 💡 Review lessons before practicing

### Scoring System
- **90-100%:** Excellent! Perfect recognition
- **80-89%:** Good! Minor improvements possible
- **70-79%:** Keep practicing
- **Below 70%:** Review lesson

## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Best Results

1. **Daily Practice**
   - Set aside 10-15 minutes daily
   - Consistency beats intensity
   - Build muscle memory

2. **Learning Path**
   - Start with Alphabet
   - Master basics before advancing
   - Don't rush through content

3. **AI Practice**
   - Practice each sign 3-5 times
   - Use good lighting
   - Follow form tips

4. **Quizzes**
   - Take quizzes after lessons
   - Review incorrect answers
   - Retake until mastery

5. **Community**
   - Ask questions
   - Share progress
   - Join challenges
   - Support others

### Common Challenges & Solutions

**Challenge:** Forgetting signs
- **Solution:** Use spaced repetition, review daily

**Challenge:** Low AI accuracy
- **Solution:** Check lighting, review lesson, practice form

**Challenge:** Feeling overwhelmed
- **Solution:** Focus on one category at a time

**Challenge:** Losing motivation
- **Solution:** Join challenges, engage with community

**Challenge:** Time constraints
- **Solution:** Even 5 minutes daily helps, use quick practice
